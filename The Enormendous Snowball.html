The Enormendous Snowball

    The winter day was perfect

    The weather was just right

    Packing snow they called it

    Had fallen the previous night



    The kids they were excited

    Dressed in mittens and in hats

    Meeting on the highest hill

    Said “Come on, let’s build it fast!”



    Starting with just a snowball

    They rolled it round and round

    All across the vast hilltop

    On the bright and snowy ground



    We will make a giant snowman

    The biggest that can be!”

    Said they grunting and pushing

    Working with industrious glee



    It grew bigger and wider

    And with each roll it became

    Far too big to push with few

    They cried “help!” and many came



    Close to the edge they rolled it

    So all in the town could see

    Got stuck and stopped moving

    They pushed, backed against a tree



    It resisted for a moment

    Moving inches but not a lot

    And then before they knew it

    It took off like a great shot



    Watching with awe and wonder

    As the snowball ran away

    Rolling faster and farther

    Growing bigger on its way



    First it hit a line of trees

    That were planted in the spring

    They were rolled into the ball

    With quite a number of things



    A few snow sleds were taken

    And skaters on the ice rink

    Workers by the deep sewers

    Even a large kitchen sink



    It rolled down into a street

    Chasing cars and Socks the stray

    Many people shouted loudly

    “Oh no! It’s coming our way”



    It kept rolling and rolling

    Taking all in its snowy fold

    Including a group of seniors

    Out for a walk in the cold



    How will we ever stop it?

    The town people cried in fear

    “No idea” said some buskers

    Let’s hope something will appear



    The kids knew what was needed

    And told the adults their plan

    Parking their cars in the road

    With one or two mini vans



    The snowball it kept rolling

    Following the path they made

    It bounded off the parked cars

    Right past the town arcade



    It turned and it swerved

    Following their clever trail

    Heading south then due north

    All hoping it would not fail



    It rounded the last corner

    The snowball racing faster

    And all did hold their breath

    Hoping for no disaster



    It crashed, totally smashed

    Against the new city hall

    Bursting itself wide open

    Lining the entire brick wall



    They had built a huge snowman

    Enormendous some did say

    It was made from everything

    On that cold and wintery day



    Then firemen did come blaring

    They pulled out their largest hose

    And from the top to the bottom

    Watered it down to wet clothes



    The kids were quite unhappy

    To see it all melt away

    But though they lost their snowman

    They went skating which was super OK
